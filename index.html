TIC TAC TOE

Welcome to Tic-Tac-Toe! This classic game is played by two players taking turns to mark a 3x3 grid. The objective is to achieve a row, column, or diagonal with three of your marks (X or O).

Game Modes:
- Player vs. Player: Play against your friend on the same device.
- Player vs. Computer: Challenge the computer to test your skills.

How to Play:
Click on an empty cell to place your mark. The game alternates between X and O. In Player vs. Computer mode, wait for the computer's move BEFORE making yours. IF you want to change gamemodes ALWAYS click the restart button to clear the board.

Winning:
Form a row, column, or diagonal with three of your marks to win. The game ends in a draw if the board is filled without a winner.

Controls:
- Click on a cell to make a move.
- Use the "Restart Game" button to start a new game.
-You can also restart when clicking the "ENTER" button when a game has finished.

Enjoy the game and may the best player win!
